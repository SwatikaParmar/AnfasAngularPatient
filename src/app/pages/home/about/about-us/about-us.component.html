About Us

Anfas Medical Care Hospital

We at Anfas are focused on the treatment of patients with long-term pulmonary problems who require mechanical ventilation and provide them with high level, quality treatment. We are also eager to take advantage of the latest medical technologies in serving our patients.

Our Mission

Providing our patients with the highest quality of care in a safe and innovative environment.

Our Vision

To be globally recognized as a leader in caring for those with chronic and long-term conditions.
